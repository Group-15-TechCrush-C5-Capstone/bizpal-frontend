Why Deals Are Lost Everyday

Small businesses struggle to track customers, follow up, and see which leads need attention.

Tracking in Notebooks

Customer details written in notebooks can get lost, damaged, or become impossible to search through as the business grows.

Lost in WhatsApp Chats

46% of Nigerian small business owners track customers through WhatsApp. Important information often gets buried and forgotten.

Forgotten Follow-ups

35% of business owners regularly forget to follow up with interested customers. Every forgotten follow-up is a lost sale.

No Pipeline Visibility

Without a system, business owners cannot see which potential customers need attention and which deals are close to closing.
